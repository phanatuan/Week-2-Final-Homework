Guessing Game

We will have a secret number, from 1 to 10. You'll have 10 guesses. If you successfully guess in fewer than 10 times, you win the game. Or else, you'll lose.

You'll have the option to restart the game at the end.

Featured:

  • After 10 clicks, the button will be disabled.
  • You can restart the game anytime, by clicking on the button "Restart The Game".
